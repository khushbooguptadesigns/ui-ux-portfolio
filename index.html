My Process

How I work

A structured, collaborative approach that brings clarity and confidence at every stage of the design journey.

01

Discover

Understand the problem space through user interviews, stakeholder workshops, and competitive research.

02

Define

Synthesize findings into clear problem statements, personas, and success metrics that guide all design decisions.

03

Design

Rapidly ideate, wireframe, and iterate — moving from rough sketches to high-fidelity interactive prototypes.

04

Deliver

Test with real users, refine based on feedback, and hand off developer-ready files with thorough documentation.
